London, Oxford Street. Seen through Marble Arch

Lovers of designer fashion have been strutting their stuff up and down London’s Bond Street (which is discreetly split into two: New and Old Bond Street) since the Georgian period. With the likes of Prada, Gucci, Alexander McQueen, Hugo Boss, Bvlgari, Cartier, Chanel, DeBeers, Armani, Hermès, Louis Vuitton, and Tod’s, there’s no luxury piece of clothing, jewellery, bag or accessory you cannot find here. Oxford Street meets New Bond Street and this is where you’ll find the world-famous Selfridges, which stands tall alongside affordable, high street shops such as Zara and H&M, souvenir shops and sport shops
